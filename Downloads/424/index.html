Learn more about Brown Bears

The brown bear is one of the largest living terrestrial carnivores. It is found across North America, Europe, and Asia.

Features of Brown Bears

  • Large and strong body
  • Thick fur coat
  • Omnivorous diet

Interesting Facts

  1. Brown bears hibernate during winter.
  2. They have an excellent sense of smell.
  3. They can run up to 40 km/h.
